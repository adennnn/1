Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How does Pickup work?
Pickup serves as an essential social platform, for people looking for quick pickup games. Our platform seamlessly links soccer & basketball players to a diverse range of local football matches, making it as simple as a click of a 3 buttons.
How do I join a game?
Once you've identified a game you wish to join, just click on the 'reserve' button. Ensure that your name is visible on the attendance list, and you're all set to get started.
Can anyone create a game?
Absolutley! You can help grow the Pickup community
